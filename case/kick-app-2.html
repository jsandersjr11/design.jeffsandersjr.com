Kick App

UI UX IxD Branding

Overview

Kick App tracks in-game player stats without taking the coach away from the action on the field.

Interviews

I conducted interviews with competitive level players and coaches. I also spoke with players parent to try and uncover hidden challenges that they are facing as they try and help their kids get to the next level.

Online Surveys

Taking the insight I received from my interviews I developed a survey with very precise questions to send out to all of my soccer club contacts. The goal of this survey was to create a baseline for where different clubs are as far as keeping players stats.

Problem Statement

Ameture athletes currently don't have the in-game information they need to take their game to the next level. The majority of teams are only tracking goals scored and sometimes assists. Currently, players are talking with college and pro recruiters without stats to back up their claims and get them to attend their games.

Wireframes

One way I like to explore possible solutions is through wireframes. The process of wireframing helped to uncover different methods for representing and displaying information as well as prioritizing that content in order of importance to the user, their expectations, and their goals.
