What is PandaOne?

PandaOne is designed to help support agents pull up Institutions information faster on Phone calls and Chats.

Interviews

I conducted several interviews with key members of the customer support team in order to gain a deeper understanding of their needs and challenges. Combining this with the time I took to observe many customer interactions I was able to gain a very deep understanding of the customer support agents pain points.

How customer support agents currently pull up information needed for incoming calls and chats.

Problem Statement

Customer support agents have a list of items they have to look up for each caller that calls in, but they don't know which Institution they are from until they receive the call. This presents that customer service agent with a huge dilemma. Do they ask the caller to wait while they open up the needed databases to help them from three-plus locations or they multitask and possibly miss important information the customer is trying to tell them that could shorten the time it would take to help them?

Wireframes

One way I like to explore possible solutions is through wireframes. The process of wireframing helped to uncover different methods for representing and displaying information as well as prioritizing that content in order of importance to the user, their expectations, and their goals.
